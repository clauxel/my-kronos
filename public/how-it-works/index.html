Process

Workflow steps

  1. Prepare candle dataConfirm symbol, market, interval, OHLC columns, optional volume or amount, timestamp order, and missing values.
  2. Choose forecast horizonSelect whether the task is short-horizon movement, volatility review, synthetic-data sanity check, or research planning.
  3. Review model assumptionsCompare data shape, market regime, evaluation gate, and risk-control expectations.
  4. Move into workspaceUse the paid plan when the team needs repeatable reviews, saved outputs, and collaboration.

Limits

Important boundaries

  • Forecasts are uncertain and should not be treated as direct trading instructions.
  • Performance depends on data quality, interval consistency, evaluation design, and market regime.
  • Financial decisions require independent review and risk controls.
  • Kronos AI is a forecasting workflow and decision-support layer, not a trading bot or guarantee of market outcomes.
